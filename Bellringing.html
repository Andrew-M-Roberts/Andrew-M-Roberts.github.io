Bellringing

Me ringing handbells at the Cambridge University Guild dinner at Wolfson College.

I currently ring towerbells regularly at Leeds Minster and St Matthias, Burley as well as other churchs more sporadically and handbells in various locations.

There are many interesting theoretical aspects of bellringing which seem to be very poorly documented online. I do not claim to be able to fully address this but may write about some of the things I notice and find interesting.
I have also recently been interested in the problem of trying to fit four “quarter peals” into a “peal”. More to follow in the hopefully near future…
