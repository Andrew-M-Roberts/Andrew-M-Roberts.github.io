Andrew Roberts

Second year PhD student at the University of Leeds under the supervision of Dr Ben Sharp. Before this I completed my BA and MMath at Gonville and Caius College, Cambridge.

I am currently interested in approximate harmonic maps, in particular ε-harmonic maps. These are constructed much like the Sacks-Uhlenbeck α-harmonic maps, by considering critical points of some approximation to the Dirichlet energy. Then one can study how these approximate harmonic maps converge as this energy converges back to the Dirichlet energy.
